ReCONNECT

Presented by Kim Schoenberger

To accompany her exhibition The C Word, Kim Schoenberger will be hosting a two-hour experimental workshop that explores the notion of the Self through personal identity and art making.

Beginning with a series of short hands-on loosening up exercises, this workshop aims to unlock pathways into the realm of creativity through the use of collage, textiles and wire armature sculpture. Each session will end with a brief dialogue in order to gain a deeper understanding in discovering your inner Creative Self. 

WORKSHOP REQUIREMENTS

  • All materials will be provided by the Artist.
  • Participants are encouraged to bring along various items of personal significance, in particular one piece of old clothing (MUST BE COTTON) which can be torn into strips, plus, a few small favourite objects, such as broken jewellery, old brooches, keys, etc.

RELATED EVENTS

  • The C word exhibition runs until Sunday 4 September, 10am-3pm daily.
  • The C word Artist Tour on Thursday 1 September, 1pm-2.30pm — register for this free event here.
