Guides to the festival: must see events

Share

Itineraries help. So, here’s a guide to help you navigate the festival.

This year’s program is big. So, we’ve created five festival itineraries (as a starting point) that are aimed at all walks of life — the first is below.

Looking for an event that says ‘I told you you’d like it?’. Look no further. Here is a guide to our must see events.

Dawn awakening

It’s a 5am start, but you wont experience anything else like it on the Sunshine Coast. Rug up and bring some coffee.

Find out more here.

Steven Oliver: Bigger & Blacker

Every been to a spiegeltent? You’re in for a treat. Here’s one of our hero events at our Festival Hub — explore the rest here.

Songs Of Gaia

An outdoor concert at Bankfoot House. It doesn’t get any more spectacular. 

Country is calling

A trip out to Maleny is always worth it. Especially for an exhibition that celebrates the people and land on which it stands.

Medicament For Your Predicament

This is an experimental pharmacy of political medicines. (We haven’t been to something like this either.)
